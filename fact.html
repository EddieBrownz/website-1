RECIPE

Avocado Toast with Poached Eggs

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe avocados
  • 4 slices of whole-grain bread
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon vinegar
  • Kosher salt and pepper to taste
  • Red pepper flakes (optional)

Steps:

  • Step 1:

    Toast the bread slices until golden brown.

  • Step 2:

    In a small pot, bring water to a simmer and add vinegar. Crack eggs into the simmering water and poach for 3-4 minutes.

  • Step 3:

    While the eggs are poaching, mash the avocados in a bowl and season with salt and pepper.

  • Step 4:

    Spread the mashed avocado on the toasted bread. Top each slice with a poached egg.

  • Step 5:

    Sprinkle with red pepper flakes if desired, and serve immediately.
